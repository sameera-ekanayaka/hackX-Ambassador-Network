## **WHAT IS hackX?**

More Than a Hackathon.

hackX is Sri Lanka's premier inter-university startup challenge, organised by the Industrial Management Science Students' Association at the Department of Industrial Management, University of Kelaniya. Since 2015, hackX has brought together the sharpest undergraduate minds from across the country to do one thing: turn real problems into scalable, investable solutions.

This is not a weekend sprint. It is a structured innovation journey through mentorship, business development, prototype building, and a Grand Finals stage in front of industry leaders, investors, and government officials.

hackX is not just for tech students. Whether your idea lives in healthcare, agriculture, finance, or education, if there is a technology-driven solution behind it, it belongs here.

## **THE JOURNEY**

Every great venture starts somewhere. Yours starts here.

**Registrations Open — June 23** Doors open to universities across Sri Lanka. Free to enter. Just bring an idea worth fighting for.

**Proposal Submission — July 31** Put your idea on paper. Teams submit structured proposals outlining the problem, the solution, and the impact. Screened by industry professionals. From this year, a one to two minute product introduction video is also required alongside the proposal.

**ideaX Semi-Finals — October 3** Thirty teams. One stage. Present a working prototype to a panel of expert judges and earn your spot at the Grand Finals.

**designX Workshops — September through October** Four expert-led sessions covering business modelling, startup structuring, and market validation. Exclusive to semi-finalists.

**Grand Finals — November 11 (Tentative)** The main event. Finalist teams present fully developed solutions before a panel of industry leaders, investors, and government officials. Cash prizes, real investor opportunities, and recognition on a national stage.

## **EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW**

**Who can register for hackX 11.0?** Any currently enrolled undergraduate from a Sri Lankan university or higher education institute. All team members must be from the same institution.

**How many people can be in a team?** Minimum two, maximum five members.

**Is there a registration fee?** No. hackX 11.0 is completely free to enter.

**Do I need a finished product to register?** No. You just need an idea at registration. A working prototype is required by the ideaX semi-finals stage.

**What do I need to submit after registering?** A structured startup proposal by July 31, along with a one to two minute product introduction video.

**Can students from non-tech faculties participate?** Absolutely. hackX welcomes innovation from any discipline. Healthcare, agriculture, finance, education, any field is fair game as long as there is a technology-driven solution behind it.

**What can winners expect at the Grand Finals?** Winners receive cash prizes, exclusive recognition on a national stage, and direct exposure to real investor opportunities through our partner network.

**What is designX?** An exclusive four-part workshop series for semi-finalists, covering business modelling, startup structuring, and market validation, delivered by industry professionals.

**When do registrations close?** Proposals must be submitted by July 31\. Register early to give your team time to prepare a strong submission.
