# **hackX 11.0 — Campus Ambassador Point Structure**


Earn cumulative referral points as your referred teams progress through the stages of hackX 11.0. 
---


## **Referral Points & Milestones Table**


| Milestone / Action | Description | Points Earned |
| :--- | :--- | :---: |
| **Successful Team Referral** | Proposal submitted with your referral ID | +100 |
| **Referred Team reaches ideaX** | Selected for the Semi-Finals round | +150 |
| **Referred Team attends designX** | Participation in mandatory workshops | +100 |
| **Referred Team reaches Grand Finals** | Selected for the Grand Finals | +250 |
| **Podium Finish Referral** | Referred team places in Top 3 | +500 |
| **Campus Awareness Event Hosted** | Co-host an orientation workshop | +300 |


---


### **Important Note**
> [!NOTE]
> Points are finalized and credited after validation of each team's milestone.
