## **INTRODUCTION: BE THE BRIDGE**

- **Eyebrow:** Be The Bridge
- **Heading:** Lead the Startup Wave on Your Campus.
- **Core Copy:**
  - As a Campus Ambassador, you become the official representative of hackX at your university. You'll connect students with opportunities, encourage innovative ideas, and help aspiring founders take their first step toward building impactful startups. 
  - More than a volunteering role, the hackX Ambassador Network is a leadership experience designed to help you grow your network, strengthen your professional profile, and gain exclusive access to opportunities within Sri Lanka's innovation ecosystem. 
- **Key Responsibilities:**
  - Represent hackX at your university
  - Promote startup culture on campus
  - Guide and support participant teams
  - Organize awareness and engagement activities

## **REWARDS & PERKS**

- **Eyebrow:** Rewards & Perks
- **Heading:** Why Join the Movement?
- **Benefits:**
  1. **Earn Referral Points:** Get rewarded for every team you bring into hackX. Earn cumulative points as your referred teams submit proposals, progress through rounds, and reach the Semi-Finals and Grand Finals. 
  2. **Climb the Leaderboard:** Compete with campus ambassadors across Sri Lanka and unlock exclusive rewards, including access to hackX workshops, VIP entry to the Grand Finals, branded merchandise, and on-stage recognition. 
  3. **Official Certification:** Successfully complete the program and receive an official digital certificate endorsed by the University of Kelaniya, Ministry of Science & Technology, and the National Science Foundation, recognizing your national-level leadership experience.
